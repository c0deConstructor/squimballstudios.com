TEXTURES (surfaces: road / ground / buildings / windows)
--------------------------------------------------------
Generated procedurally in-page (HTML canvas) at runtime. No external image
files — nothing to download or host. See pit_game_nextgen.html.

AUDIO
-----
Synthesized procedurally in-browser via the Web Audio API (oscillators +
filtered noise). No external audio files. Optionally override with your own
samples placed in audio/ (see audio/README.txt).

NOTE ON SAFETY: .glb (models) and .png (images) are inert data formats. They
contain geometry/pixels only — no scripts or executable code. Three.js parses
them strictly as data.
